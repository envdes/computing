Overview

Computing Resources

Members of our lab have access to the following High Performance Computing (HPC) resources:

  • ARCHER2 (a world class advanced computing resource for UK researchers)

  • Bede (the N8 CIR’s new high performance computing platform)

  • CSF (Computational Shared Facility, the HPC cluster at The University of Manchester, ~8,644 cores + 100 GPUs)

We also have an in-house GPU workstation (NVIDIA® RTXTM A5000, 24 GB GDDR6)
